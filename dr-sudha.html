Conditions Treated

Comprehensive care for common and complex anorectal problems.

Piles (Hemorrhoids)

  • Bleeding while passing stools
  • Pain, swelling or lumps around the anus
  • Itching, discomfort and difficulty sitting

Anal Fissure

  • Severe pain during and after passing stool
  • Fresh blood on stool or tissue
  • Burning or tearing sensation at anal opening

Anal Fistula

  • Small openings or boils near the anus
  • Repeated pus discharge or infection
  • Persistent pain and swelling

Pilonidal Sinus & Other Conditions

  • Pilonidal sinus near tailbone
  • Perianal abscess
  • Chronic anal pain & recurrent infections

When Should You Consult Dr. Sudha?

Do not ignore symptoms related to the anal region out of shyness or fear. Early consultation often helps avoid complications and major surgery.
  • Bleeding during or after passing stool
  • Persistent pain, burning or itching around the anus
  • Lumps, swelling or repeated boils near the anal region
  • Pus or fluid discharge near the anus
  • Chronic constipation or difficulty in passing stool
